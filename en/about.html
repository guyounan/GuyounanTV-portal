About Us

WHO ARE WE？

Guyonan TV is an Indonesian digital production and content creator, founded on October 12th, 2016 and based in Jakarta.

In accordance with our name, Guyonan, which means “jokes” in Javanese, our mission is to entertain people with our original funny contents. As we grow, we also create videos on other genres, although we still stay on our comedy elements on each video.

We have produced more than thousand original videos. Our professional production team created all the content by themselves, from the script to post-production.

Guyonan TV’s videos are published mainly in our official Instagram and Youtube account. Besides, we also posted our videos on various social media platform, such as Facebook, UC Browser, etc.

  • Instagram followers reached 250K++
  • Huge sources of talents and cewebrities.
  • Professional production and creative team.
  • Experienced social media specialist and product operation team.
  • Cooperation with other short video platforms.
